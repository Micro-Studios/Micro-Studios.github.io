Hello, I am Michael HU.

I am a big fan of technology. I truly believe, technology is an art. I love designing all kinds of electronic devices. I am not only interested in hardware such as circuit design, PCB layout, but also different firmware software programming.

I strongly agree that hardware is the body, and software is the soul, a technological art work is the perfect combination of the two.

I love sharing my knowledge and experience. George Bernard Shaw said:

“If you have an apple and I have an apple and we exchange these apples then you and I will still each have one apple. But if you have an idea and I have an idea and we exchange these ideas, then each of us will have two ideas.”

This is why I created the micro-studios website. It is a place where I can share my knowledge and ideas, and I would also like to learn about yours. Let us enjoy and explore the beauty of technology.
